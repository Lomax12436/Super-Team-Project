Create your own Dungeons and Dragons Character!

This application can be used to help you create a Dungeons and Dragons character! Already have and idea? Click the "Build your own" button. Need an idea from scratch? Click the "Generate Character" button.

Our mission is to make Dungeons and Dragons more accessable to new or more experienced players. This application can be used by a player to get new ideas and switch up the rhythm of your normal playing habits or it can be used by the DM to create interested and complex NPCs on the spot that can be an unforgettable asset to your players!
